# Pytalon Code of Conduct

## 🌟 Welcome!

Hey there! 👋 The Pytalon community is a friendly mix of professionals, volunteers, and learners like you from all around the world. We're here to mentor, teach, connect, and grow together.

**You belong here.** Whether you're a beginner writing your first line of code or an experienced developer helping others level up — this space was built for you.

## 💝 Our Simple Promise to You

Pytalon is more than a learning tool — it's a community. And we want it to be a **safe, welcoming, and encouraging** place where:

- Questions are celebrated, never judged
- Mistakes are stepping stones, not setbacks
- Kindness is always the default setting
- Every learner feels seen and supported

## 📋 A Few Ground Rules (Just the Essentials)

Think of these as good vibes we all agree to bring with us:

### ✅ Be Friendly & Patient

We all start somewhere. That "simple" question might be someone's biggest breakthrough today. Treat it that way.

### ✅ Be Welcoming

You are welcome here regardless of your:

- Race, ethnicity, or culture
- Gender identity or expression
- Age or education level
- Background or beliefs
- Experience with Python (zero or hero — both are awesome!)

### ✅ Be Considerate

Your code, comments, and words affect real people. Pause before you post. Ask yourself: *"Would I say this to someone sitting right next to me?"*

### ✅ Be Respectful

Disagreements happen — and that's totally fine! But let's disagree without being disagreeable. Challenge ideas, never people.

### ✅ Choose Your Words Kindly

We're all professionals here (or becoming one!). That means:

- No threats or violence
- No hate speech or discriminatory jokes
- No sharing personal information without permission ("doxing")
- No harassment of any kind
- If someone asks you to stop — just stop. No questions asked.

### ✅ Try to Understand, Not Just Win

We come from different places, speak different languages, and see the world differently. That's our superpower! When you disagree with someone, ask yourself: *"What can I learn from this perspective?"*

## 🚨 Something Feel Off? Let Us Know

If you see or experience anything that doesn't feel right — even if you're not 100% sure — please **reach out**. Your voice matters here.

📧 **Email:** acuburagamer100@gmail.com

Every report will be:

- Taken seriously
- Handled with full privacy
- Investigated fairly and thoroughly
- Kept completely confidential

You will never get in trouble for speaking up. You'll be making Pytalon safer and better for everyone who comes after you.

## 🌍 Where These Rules Apply

This Code of Conduct applies across all Pytalon spaces:

- GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Any chat rooms, emails, or forums we create
- Community events (online or in person)
- Even outside spaces, if behavior directly affects the safety of our community

## 💬 Questions or Just Want to Talk?

No question is too small. No concern is too minor. If something isn't clear — or you just need someone to talk to — **we're here.**
